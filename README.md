## Swiftcart Online Store Sales Analysis
Excel-based sales analysis of Swiftcart Online Store covering 50 orders across 5 Indian cities. Built using Pivot Tables, Charts, and an interactive Dashboard with slicers.

📁 Dataset

Records: 50 orders | Columns: 13
Categories: Electronics, Fashion, Beauty, Furniture
Cities: Mumbai, Delhi, Pune, Chennai, Kolkata
Payment Methods: UPI, COD, Card

🔍 Key Insights

👫 Equal split — 50% Male & 50% Female customers
🏙️ Top cities by sales: Mumbai, Delhi & Chennai
💳 Card & UPI are the most used payment methods
✅ Majority of orders successfully Delivered
🛍️ Electronics & Fashion are top revenue categories
